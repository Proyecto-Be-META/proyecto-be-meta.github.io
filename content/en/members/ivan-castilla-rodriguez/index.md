Iván Castilla Rodríguez is an Associate Professor (Profesor Titular) at the Universidad de La Laguna in the Department of Computer Engineering and Systems. He received his Ph.D. in 2010 with a thesis on parallel simulation in multi-core systems, and his research integrates discrete-event simulation, artificial intelligence, and semantic models.

His academic output includes around 40 articles in indexed journals and close to 50 conference contributions. His main areas of application are: health, through the development of models for the economic evaluation of health technologies, and AI- and semantic-model-based tools to support every phase of building these models; energy, with research on renewable planning and energy storage; logistics, with the modeling of management in infrastructures (ports, airports, and hospitals); and computer architecture education, with the development of teaching tools such as the SIMDE simulator.
