Since 2001 she has been a faculty member in the area of Languages and Computer Systems at the Universidad de La Laguna, holding the position of Profesora Contratada Doctora (type 1) since 2007. For two years (2000-2002) she took part in the development of various projects for the Department of Employment and Social Affairs of the Government of the Canary Islands.
She holds a Ph.D. from the Universidad de La Laguna, with the thesis Parallel Computing and Heterogeneous Environments (2005). She has been a member of the research groups Parallel Computing Group and TARO Group: software engineering and databases, co-authoring several research articles in journals and publications presented at scientific-technological and teaching-innovation conferences.
She is currently a member of the Cultural Classroom of Computational Thinking at the Universidad de La Laguna and participates in the Be-META research project (Beyond Metaheuristics: A Reference Framework for Visual Modeling of Problems and Algorithms in the Field of Hospital Management).
