He began his career in the Department of Computer Engineering and Systems at the ULL in the 2012/2013 academic year, with a University Faculty Training (FPU) fellowship in the areas of Systems Engineering and Automation / Computer Architecture and Technology, and as a researcher in the ULL Robotics Group. Upon its completion, he joined the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence area under a temporary employment contract. After a brief period as a Junior Programmer at Simloc Research Flight Simulation and Ilion Animation Studios, he obtained a position as Assistant Professor (Profesor Ayudante Doctor) in the area of Languages and Computer Systems.
